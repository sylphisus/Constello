# Ideas to implement

## Birthdays on Constello

Not that I've ever been a fan of astrology or anything, but I saw a tiktok about how people pass their birthdays as any other day and having our app honor the birth of their existence is very constello-esque. We have no identifying parameters on constello, no names or race or height or age, but we might just have birthdays.

## Referral connection as in-app achievement

Attach an in-app achievement to people's referral links, so that if the person that made an account off that link connects with the person who generated that link, they get an achievement. It's an interesting game and would attract users because people are naturally curious if they would connect with particular people. This matters to them.

Also consider adding a special achievement for connecting with me, the founder, as an easter egg. Drives more attention when something can be hunted for like that, good virality mechanics when there's mystery and people have to solve things themselves. People love finding out about things they think they're not supposed to know.
