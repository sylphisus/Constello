03 is the parent. @03wastaken is the brand's main handle and hub — personal for now, but ultimately the top of the house — and @03constello, @03[next], and the rest are derivatives. The 03 prefix is the family marker: anything with 03 in front reads as one house, so a new property inherits the recognition instead of starting cold.

The handle is also the thesis applied to its own name. A premium OG handle is a surface token — manufactured scarcity the attention economy prices and everyone agrees is worth something. Constello's reading move is to ignore the surface token and read the world built on it[^reading]. So building a real brand on a deliberately "worthless" handle is that move turned on itself:

> dropping 100 bands on the ogu is sooo tuff and is all what constello is about. Ive written in the rebellion, dont you see? The handle itself is a denial of the attention economy—"fuck your nice handles theyre worthless to me." Ive created this whole brand on top of something "stupid" in your world instead. As constello grows in spite of the handle, it jointly serves to deconstruct the religion weve formed for the sake of attention

The handle isn't a compromise that contradicts the brand; it's the brand's argument made in its own URL.

How it does the work matters: through the trajectory, not through being explained. "Grows in spite of the handle" only becomes visible once there's growth to contrast against, so the deconstruction is retroactive — on day one @03wastaken reads as someone who couldn't get a handle, and it's the success on top of it that converts it, after the fact, into the point. So the rule is never to caption it. The moment it's explained ("this is a denial of the attention economy") it collapses into a bit and loses what made it real. You don't announce it; you can just tell.

The skeptic's read — that this is post-hoc rationalizing of a constraint you couldn't afford around — doesn't land, because origin doesn't change the structure. Whether it started as constraint or choice, building-and-succeeding on a worthless token demonstrates the thesis either way. And "the material was a constraint, the world built on it is the point" is itself the thesis — so even the constraint-reframed-as-principle is on-register. The only rebuttal to the skeptic is the trajectory, which is the same answer as the rule above.
